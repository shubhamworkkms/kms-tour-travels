Explore Kashmir

From silent crystal-clear lakes to roaring mountain rivers, discover the unique character of each region in the Paradise on Earth.

Srinagar - The Summer Capital

Ideal Duration: 2-3 Days Best Time: March to October

Srinagar lies in the Kashmir Valley on the banks of the Jhelum River. It is famous for its houseboats, historic Mughal gardens (Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, Chashme Shahi), and floating flower markets on Dal Lake. Witness the majestic Shankaracharya Temple overlooking the city.

Top Attractions: Dal Lake Shikara Ride, Nigeen Lake, Shalimar Gardens, Tulip Garden (Seasonal), Pari Mahal, Nehru Park, Lal Chowk handicraft shopping.

Gulmarg - The Meadow of Flowers

Ideal Duration: 1-2 Days Best Time: December to March (for Snow)

Gulmarg is the ski capital of Asia, nestled in the Pir Panjal Range. In spring and summer, it is carpeted with wildflowers, while winters turn it into a world-class skiing hub. It hosts the Gulmarg Gondola, one of the highest cable cars in the world, climbing to nearly 4000 meters at Apharwat Peak.

Top Attractions: Gondola Ride (Phase 1 & 2), Alpather Lake, Snow Point activities, Historic Golf Course, St. Mary's Church, Tangmarg drive.

Pahalgam - The Valley of Shepherds

Ideal Duration: 2 Days Best Time: April to November

Pahalgam sits along the banks of the Lidder River and serves as the starting point for the annual Amarnath Yatra pilgrimage. Known for its pine forests, coniferous meadows, and rocky river paths, it offers fantastic angling, trekking, and horse riding.

Top Attractions: Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, Chandanwari, Baisaran Valley (Mini Switzerland), Lidder River rafting, Apple Orchards.

Sonamarg - The Meadow of Gold

Ideal Duration: 1 Day Excursion Best Time: April to October

Sonamarg is surrounded by snowy glaciers and alpine lakes. The scenic Sindh River winds through it, offering white-water rafting opportunities. It acts as the gateway to Ladakh via the famous, thrilling Zojila Pass.

Top Attractions: Thajiwas Glacier trek, Sindh Valley streams, Zero Point off-roading, Zoji La Pass, alpine trekking routes.

Find Sonamarg Packages

Gurez Valley - The Hidden Paradise

Ideal Duration: 2 Days (with Camping) Best Time: June to September

Gurez Valley is an extremely off-beat valley located near the Line of Control. Guarded by the imposing, pyramid-shaped Habba Khatoon Peak, Gurez is populated by the Dard-Shin tribe. It features log huts, green meadows, and the beautiful river Kishenganga. Perfect for quiet camping and trekking away from tourist crowds.

Top Attractions: Habba Khatoon Peak and natural spring, Razdan Pass (3550m), Kishenganga riverside camping, Dard-Shin cultural villages.
