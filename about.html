Your Trusted Local Kashmir Experts

At KMS Tour & Travels, we believe that traveling should be personal, local, and stress-free. Established directly in Srinagar, our team is comprised of local Kashmiri travel coordinators, professional transport drivers, and native guides who know the hidden meadows and high-altitude passes better than anyone.

We have built long-standing networks with local houseboats, boutique hotel owners, and adventure camping vendors to offer high-quality service at transparent, budget-friendly rates. From honeymoons to school adventure groups, we tailor every itinerary to suit your pace.

Our Vision

To represent authentic Kashmiri hospitality globally and provide every traveler with deep memories that last forever.

Our Mission

To deliver safe, customized, and value-packed tour packages while supporting local communities, drivers, and hoteliers.

What Sets Us Apart

Why thousands of families and couples trust their Kashmir plans to KMS Tour & Travels.

Customized Flex-Itineraries

We do not run rigid group schedules. If you want to spend extra hours in Gulmarg snow or relax by Lidder River, our cab drivers adapt dynamically.

Local Rates, No Middlemen

Since we operate from Srinagar and own our cabs/SUVs, you save up to 25% by cutting out third-party booking agents in major metro cities.

Safe & Secure Tourism

We keep real-time track of road alerts, Zojila blockages, border permits for Gurez, and weather checks to ensure your family's safety is never compromised.
